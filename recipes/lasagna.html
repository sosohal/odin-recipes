Lalalasagna

Description:

This is a lasagna recipe. It probably originiated from Italy. They sure love pasta. Or maybe it's an American thing? Since it tastes so good, it probably is ha. Good luck darling.

Ingredients:
  • lasagna pasta
  • cheese
  • pasta sauce
  • basil
  • salt

Steps:

  1. Boil water and add salt. Then add pasta.
  2. Drain the pasta.
  3. Layer pasta then sauce then cheese and basil.
  4. Bake in the oven for some amount of time.
  5. Eat it out of the oven as it was intended by Mr. Lalalasagna.
